How to make Chocolate Fondue

Ingredients!

  • chopped chocolate or chocolate chips
  • milk
  • cream
  • vanilla extract
  • fruits
  • sweets

For the Fondue

  1. Combine everything but the vanilla extract in a saucepan
  2. Place the pot of fondue ingredients over low heat and stir occasionally while the chocolate melts. When fully melted, the fondue will be glossy, thick and smooth!
  3. Take off the heat, stir in the vanilla extract, amount is to your desire.

    4. SPARKLE NOTES

  4. This recipe makes a thick, luscious chocolate fondue.
  5. If you want the fondue a bit thinner. Simply whisk in additional cream or milk until the desired consistency is reached~
  6. if you want the fondue a bit thicker. Simply add more in chocolate~
