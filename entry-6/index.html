How to make Fresh Berry Tart with Vanilla Cream

Ingredients!

  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Pecans
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cream cheese
  • Confectioners’ sugar
  • Lemons
  • Strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, raspberries

Fresh Berry Tart with Vanilla Cream (Non-bake or bake Ver)

  1. Add all crust ingredients in a food processor and pulse until well combined
  2. Press firmly into tart tins.
  3. If you want to leave this as a no-bake crust, set in fridge for 1 hour. If you would like to bake it, preheat oven to 350° and bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned.
  4. Using a mixer or electric beater, mix together the cream cheese and powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Stir in Cool Whip and almond extract until well combined.
  6. Spread evenly over prepared crust and top with fresh fruit.
  7. Let it set in the fridge for at least an hour.
  8. Cut into slices and serve.
