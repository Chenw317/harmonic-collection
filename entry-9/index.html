Smoky Charcoal Pizza with Black Olives and Truffle Oil: Fear

Fear is a natural and strong emotion that arises when you perceive a threat or danger. It can be both real or imagined. It is a survival mechanism in our brain that is designed to protect you by triggering a "fight, flight, or freeze" response, it prepares our body to react to potential harm.

Physically fear exemplifies itself through increased heart rate, rapid breathing, muscle tension, and sweating or chills. Psychologically it heightens awareness, sharpens focus on the threat, and can leave you feeling overwhelmed or paralyzed. While fear is essential for survival and self-preservation, excessive or irrational fear such as in phobias or anxiety disorders. This can lead to and become limiting and interfere with daily life.
